❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the baggage allowance for students? ▼

Students get an extra 10kg baggage allowance on top of the standard baggage. This is part of our student special fares which also includes a 25% discount on ticket prices. Valid student ID is required at check-in.

What discounts do armed forces personnel get? ▼

Armed forces personnel get a 15% discount on all flights, plus priority boarding and faster check-in. Valid military ID is required at the counter. This applies to active personnel and veterans with proper documentation.

Are senior citizens eligible for discounts? ▼

Yes! Senior citizens (60+ years) receive a 10% discount on all fares. There is also complimentary assistance available for boarding and deplaning. A valid ID proof with date of birth is required at check-in.

What is the cancellation policy? ▼

Full refund if cancelled 7 days before departure. 50% refund for cancellations between 3-7 days. No refund within 72 hours of departure. However, you can modify your flight up to 24 hours before departure for a nominal fee.

How do I book a multi-city flight? ▼

Select the 'Multi-City' option in our booking form. You can then add multiple legs of your journey. For each leg, specify your departure and arrival cities along with the date. You'll get the best prices for combined bookings.

What payment methods are accepted? ▼

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, net banking, and digital wallets like Google Pay, Apple Pay, and Paytm. Payment is secure and encrypted. You'll receive a confirmation email immediately after successful payment.

Can I change my flight after booking? ▼

Yes, you can change your flight up to 24 hours before departure. A nominal fee of ₹500-1000 applies depending on availability and price difference. Changes can be made online through your booking confirmation or by contacting customer service.

Is travel insurance available? ▼

Travel insurance is optional and can be purchased during checkout. It covers medical emergencies, trip cancellations, and lost baggage. Insurance premiums start from ₹99 per trip. We recommend it for international flights.

How do I track my booking? ▼

Log into your FlightBook account using your email and password. You can view all your bookings, check flight status, and manage your reservations from the "My Bookings" section. You'll also receive real-time updates via SMS and email.

How can I contact customer support? ▼

Our customer support team is available 24/7 through multiple channels. Call us at +91-9876-543-210, email support@flightbook.com, or use the live chat feature on our website. We typically respond within 5 minutes.
